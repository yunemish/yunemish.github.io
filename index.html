Yuriy Nemish

Since Fall 2019 I'm an SEW Visiting Assistant Professor at UC San Diego (Department of Mathematics). My sponsor is Todd Kemp. I'm working on the problems of universality for random matrices. Before coming to UCSD, I was a postdoc in the research group of Lásló Erdős at the Institute of Science and Technology Austria (2016-2019). I did my PhD at Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse under the supervision of Mireille Capitaine and Michel Ledoux.
